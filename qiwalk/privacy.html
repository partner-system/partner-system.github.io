Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how QiWalk ("the App") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

The App accesses your location to display maps, calculate directions, and provide route navigation. Location data is processed on your device only and is not transmitted to external servers.

Camera

The App accesses your camera for the AR compass feature. Camera footage is not stored or transmitted.

Purchase Information

Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, which uses anonymous identifiers. No personally identifiable information is collected through this service.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely for:

1. Displaying compass lines and calculating directions on the map.

2. Providing AR compass directional overlay.

3. Route navigation functionality.

4. Managing subscription status.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

1. With your consent.

2. As required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

1. Google Maps - For map display.

2. RevenueCat - For subscription management.

3. Apple App Store / Google Play - For app distribution and payment processing.

Each service has its own privacy policy governing its use of data.

5. Data Storage

App data (favorite locations, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit or store data on our servers.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes take effect when posted on this page.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@abturning.com
